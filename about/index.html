Our Story

From dealership experience to automation innovation

What is GearShift? ⚙️

During the summers I had internships or worked at my family's dealership, where I have been working at since before I could drive. Doing everything from washing cars to buying them.

Last summer when I was working as a customer service person I couldn't help but recognize how useful the application for an AI agent would be in that sector.

After learning that automation tools are in continuous development and the dealer world was untapped in that regard I got to work. I started building my passion project GearShift, a long-term project bringing automation tools to dealers, giving dealerships more efficient communication methods.
